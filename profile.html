About Me

A passion for people, polished by practice.

I'm Jordan — a customer service specialist who believes that great support is less about scripts and more about showing up. Over the last five years, I've helped startups and growing e-commerce brands transform their customer experience from a cost center into a competitive advantage.

My approach blends genuine empathy with operational rigor: I listen first, solve fast, and then zoom out to fix the root cause so the same issue doesn't resurface. I've built help centers from scratch, trained support teams across three time zones, and turned angry one-star reviewers into brand evangelists. Whether it's a single ticket or a thousand, every customer deserves to feel heard.
